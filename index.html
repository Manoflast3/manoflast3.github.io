Stuff I'm currently working on:

Solenoid Actuated Microrobot (SAM)

SAM is an ongoing project to design a micro robot which uses solenoids to move iron-oxide particles. I am the lead electrical designer responsible for designing the PCB.

Wriber

Wriber is a idea generator aimed at helping writers write better content. I am currently improving Wriber's natural language processing ability and the quality of its idea generation.

Surface Acoustic Wave Microrobot (SAW)

SAW is an experimental, fluid micro robot which will be actuated by surface acoustic waves produced on a piezoelectric substrate. I have completed the prototype of the microrobot, with a schematic viewable online.
